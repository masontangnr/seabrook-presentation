Web Design

Components to a Good Website

1

Describe What Your Company Does

Visitors should know within seconds what you offer and who it's for — clarity wins every time.

2

Strong Call To Action

Tell visitors exactly what to do next — "Book a Session," "Call Now," or "Get Started Today."

3

About Me / About Company Page

People do business with people they trust. Share your story, background, and why you do what you do.

4

Testimonials

Real reviews from happy clients build instant credibility — let your students and parents do the talking.

5

Ways for Customers to Contact You

Email, phone, contact form — make it easy for potential clients to reach you in whatever way they prefer.
